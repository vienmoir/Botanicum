Fagus sylvatica, the European beech or common beech, is a deciduous tree belonging to the beech family Fagaceae.
Fagus sylvatica is a large tree, capable of reaching heights of up to 50 m tall and 3 m trunk diameter, though more typically 25–35 m tall and up to 1.5 m trunk diameter.
