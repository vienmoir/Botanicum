Fraxinus excelsior, known as the ash, or European ash or common ash, is a flowering plant species in the olive family Oleaceae. 
It is a large deciduous tree growing to 12–18 m tall with a trunk up to 2 m diameter, with a tall, narrow crown. The bark is smooth and pale grey on young trees, becoming thick and vertically fissured on old trees.
