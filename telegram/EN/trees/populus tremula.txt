Populus tremula, commonly called aspen, common aspen, Eurasian aspen, European aspen, or quaking aspen, is a species of poplar native to cool temperate regions of Europe and Asia.
It is a substantial deciduous tree growing to 40 m tall by 10 m broad, with a trunk attaining over 1 m in diameter. The bark is pale greenish-grey and smooth on young trees with dark grey diamond-shaped lenticels.
